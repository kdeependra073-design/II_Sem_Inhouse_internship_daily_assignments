About Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Army, and its professional head is the Chief of Army Staff.

Our Core Regiments

Infantry

The combat arm that engages with the enemy face-to-face. Known as the backbone of the army.

Artillery

The arm of long-range firepower. Delivering devastating support to troops on the ground.

Armoured Corps

The punch of the army, utilizing advanced tanks and mechanized power to breach frontline enemy defenses.
